Overview

The connection between humans and music is undeniable. Perhaps this is due, in part, to the way music evokes our emotions, or maybe it’s about embodied response. People share music to feel connected. Spotify wants to make a move into helping that connection further. I designed a new social feature that embeds within the current Spotify platform in any device of your choice and runs smoothly with the rest of the app.

The Challenge

Spotify is not legally available in India. People listen to music on several different platforms (Youtube, Wynk, Apple Music) and devices. Everyone has a favorite. This makes sharing music with others especially difficult. Sharing music needs to be more real time, involving discoveries and conversations.

The Solution

WhatsApp is the most popular app used for sharing and communication. Most people share music/video links on Whatsapp. The process of clicking on the link, moving to another app, installing the app (if available), listening to the song, switching back to Whatsapp and commenting on the shared song can be extremely frustrating.
I decided to combine Spotify and Whatsapp into a new feature - Spotify-bot. Spotifybot is a Google Assistant like mini-Spotify that lives in Whatsapp. Some useful features:

  1. No more juggling between apps If a Spotify link is shared, spotify-bot immediately picks it up and music is played in-house. This makes it easier to share and talk about music
  2. Common Platform You don’t need Spotify installed to listen to the link being shared.
  3. Real-time Sharing You are notified when the other person listens to the song you sent - this makes the sharing experience more real time and helps create relevant conversations
  4. Personalized recommendations Spotifybot suggests songs from your playlist you can share

Defining the user

Based on my research, I created a user persona and empathy map to understand the user’s goals, needs and behaviors.

Ideation

I used the ‘Crazy Eights’ method, 8 ideas were generated in 8 mixtures for each of the strongest PoVs. This was then repeated several times, each resulting in additional ideals or improvements.

I used the crazy-8s method to quickly ideate on my focus PoVs. I sketched 8 ideas each in 8 minutes. I repeated this process and worked on refining my favorites.

I created a storyboard to help visually predict and explore my persona’s experience with the product.

Strategy

I created a user flow, from my storyboard. I follow two user flows for:

  1. The Sharer Sam - Who shares the link
  2. The Sharee Neha - Who listens to the link
